About Us

Barelang MRT is a robot team from Politeknik Negeri Batam which focuses on developing surface and underwater ship robots. Barelang MRT has two different divisions with the first division, namely Barelang MRT - Nereus, which is responsible for developing surface ship robots and the second division, namely the Underwater Division, which is responsible for developing underwater ship robots in the form of Autonomous Underwater Vessels.

National Competition

The Barelang MRT team also participated in several national competitions such as the National Unmanned Fast Boat Contest (KKCTBN) and the Indonesian Underwater Robot Contest (KRI). For the National Unmanned Fast Boat Contest (KKCTBN) has the same concept as the RoboBoat. While the Indonesian Underwater Robot Contest (KRI) is a contest of robots that can move underwater.

International Competition

The Barelang MRT team participated in several competitions such as RoboBoat which is an international competition held every year in Florida, USA. For this reason, the Barelang MRT team was formed to take part in its first competition, RoboBoat 2022. Until now, the Barelang MRT team has participated twice in international competitions start from RoboBoat 2022 to RoboBoat 2023.
