About Us

Barelang MRT is a robot team from Politeknik Negeri Batam which focuses on developing surface and underwater ship robots. Barelang MRT has two different divisions with the first division, namely Barelang MRT - Nereus, which is responsible for developing surface ship robots and the second division, namely the Underwater Division, which is responsible for developing underwater ship robots in the form of Autonomous Underwater Vessels.
